ℹ️ About Human Year Conversions: Each animal type has a different life expectancy, so we use species-specific formulas to convert their age to the human equivalent. For example, a 2-year-old dog ≈ 24 human years, a 1-year-old hamster ≈ 25 human years, and a 3-year-old rabbit ≈ 25 human years. The Human B-Day shown on each pet card is the date that would correspond to their age if they were human — so you can see both their real birthday and their human-equivalent milestone on the calendar!
